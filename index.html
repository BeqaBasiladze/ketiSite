Keti Diakonidze
Originally from Moscow, Russia, Keti Diakonidze studied interior design and graphic design at Istituto Design Palladio in Verona. As a designer, she's feed on art and new ideas. Moreover, Keti Diakonidze, starts each project by creating a strong connection with the client that will interact with her designs, establishing the “focal elements and point”. For her is very important to think spaces or objects in relation to people. Keti Diakonidze has worked for Brandless Studio, Edilvetta and she collaborates with several architects of Verona.

Prizes and Awards
She has won the Silver Prize of MM Award 2021.

Languages
You can contact her from anywhere in the world because she speaks and understands six different languages icluding russian, german, spanish, georgian, italian and of coure, english. This because she aspires to work at an international level without borders and limits.
